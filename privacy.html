Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

AGI Legal Pro ("we", "us", or "our") operates as a global affidavit generation platform. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Google Account Data: When you log in with Google, we receive your email address and public display name. We do not see or store your Google password.
  • Affidavit Content: The details you enter (full name, father's name, age, address, purpose, statement, etc.) are stored in our secure cloud database (Firebase Firestore) in order to generate and finalize your affidavit.
  • Signature & Photo: Any signature you draw or photo you upload is stored as part of your affidavit document.
  • Payment Information: We never store your credit/debit card or UPI details. All payments are processed securely by Razorpay, a PCI‑DSS compliant gateway. We only keep a transaction reference and order ID for audit purposes.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for:

  • Generating, previewing, and finalizing affidavits
  • Managing your premium subscription (if purchased)
  • Enabling QR‑based public verification (only the affidavit’s existence and status are public – never your personal details)
  • Improving the app experience (themes, preferences)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • All affidavit and profile data is stored on Google Firebase (Firestore), with encryption at rest and in transit.
  • Access to your documents is restricted to your authenticated account only (enforced by Firestore security rules).
  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Razorpay, and we never see your full card details.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your affidavits and account data until you request deletion. To request deletion of all your data, contact us at support@yourdomain.com. We will process your request within 30 days.

5. Public Verification

When you finalize an affidavit, a unique, unguessable verification token is generated. Scanning the QR code opens a public page that shows only whether the document is valid and its status (Active/Revoked). It does not display your name, address, or any personal information.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your profile information
  • Request deletion of your affidavits and account data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (logout and stop using the app)

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
