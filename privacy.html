Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2025

All affidavit data is stored locally on your device using IndexedDB and localStorage. No personal information is sent to our servers unless you manually export or share it.

Data Collection

We collect only the information you provide in the affidavit forms (name, address, signature, photo, etc.). This data remains on your device.

Third‑Party Services

We use locally hosted libraries (html2pdf, html2canvas, QRCode, SignaturePad, DOMPurify) – no external calls except for Google Fonts (which can be disabled if needed).

Your Rights

You can clear all stored data at any time by clearing your browser cache / IndexedDB. Use the browser's developer tools or site settings.
